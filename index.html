HARDWOOD AUCTION

A two-player NBA drafting duel. Spin the reel, stop on a legend, then out-bid your rival to build the better roster.
How it works
  • Each of you starts with 25 coins and an empty roster: PG · SG · SF · PF · C.
  • Every round the reel cycles well-known All-Stars for one position. The designated player hits STOP — it slows and lands on a name.
  • An auction opens. The player who stopped it gets a 5-second head start to bid before their rival can jump in.
  • Tap to raise. Highest bid when the clock runs out wins the player, pays the coins, and locks them into that roster slot.
  • Exactly 2 of each position appear across 10 rounds, so both rosters fill up. You can't bid on a slot you've already filled.
  • At the end, the roster with the highest total rating wins. Spend wisely.
Best played on one shared screen — two rivals, one device.
